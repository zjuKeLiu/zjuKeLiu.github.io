Biography

I'm currently a third-year Ph.D student of the College of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University, fortunately advised by Prof. Chunhua Shen. Before that I got my bachelor's degree from Zhejiang University in july, 2021 majoring in Automation and minoring in Advanced-honor Class of Engineering Education(ACEE), Chu Kochen honors College(CKC).
